Digital Zines!!!

Published on: July 22, 2025

So I have stubbled upon this awesome thing that I've seen growing up and around artsy spaces but never considered trying myself! ZINES!!!!! That realization had blew my mind because I had absolutely no idea how fun and exciting it was to be able to create, put ideas out into the world, the print/fold/have a physical thing I made fully by myself.

I don't even remember how I stumbled upon this video of a lady making and educating about zines and it felt like the thing I needed to do instantly!! Perfect thing to do that I've been looking for to keep me busy when I'm between projects.

So here is where I will be posting my zines.

TAKE CONTROL ISSUE. 1

This contains some artworks I've made, plus just some awesome interesting facts that some people may not know. Even if you do, Always nice to have a reference sheet/reminder!

Thank you for taking your time to read this. I plan to make these regularly as a way to make art and not always have to focus on quality
