Y2K Inspired Logos|

Overview|

Date: August 19, 2024

Y2k aesthetic inspired Logos I made for myself and other artists to promote my buisness of selling graphic design services.

*The Y2k Aesthetic is based on 2000s fashion, pop culture and technology.*

100 gecs, Bladeee, Fraxiom, Dorian Electra. Just to name a few huge inspirations along this jorurney.

Contributions:|

I incorporated the add into the logos themselves to show the service but also the flexibility in what is actually possible and what can be done. It allowed many variations on different looking text blocks, opportunity to provide variety in the logo designs aswell.

Motivation:|

I very much enjoy this style of art and want to make it easy for more people to use and involve themselves with. It is something that I can do well and can offer to others who may not know how or want to create it themselves.

Development:|

Use of Grungy Texture layers to present a rough, used look. Commonly used Bold Outlines. Contains curvy, sharp and/or angular shapes.
