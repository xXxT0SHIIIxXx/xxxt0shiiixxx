xXx_T0SHIII_xXx|

Overview|

Date: August 19, 2024

xXx_T0SHIII_xXx is my Hyperpop/Scenecore/Digicore artist alias. I make art and music under that name. Very Spastic, Colorful, rooted within 2000s alternative internet culture and alternative aesthetics.

In Early 2020, I was introduced to Hyperpop, Scenecore, and Digicore music through a friend wanting to poke fun at the outrageous visuals and sounds. I fell in love ever since.

100 gecs, Bladeee, Fraxiom, Dorian Electra. Just to name a few huge inspirations along this jorurney.

Contributions:|

I do everything except mixing/mastering for this project. I do all the branding, music, artwork, website coding. I produce a lot of my own instrumentals whilst doing vocals aswell.

One of my biggest contributions for this project is this website.

Motivation:|

The genre/scene perfectly fits my interests, it speaks to me on a different level than other genres that I like. This project is more about having fun and exploring music sonically than making any sort of statements or voicing opinions.

Development:|

Very heavy use of audio processing techniques like realtime pitch shifting vocals. Use of Serum to produce mind-bending and melodic synth pieces. Mix of Hardcore and Easy going sound profiles.
