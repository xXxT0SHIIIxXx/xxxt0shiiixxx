Euphobia|

Overview|

Date: August 9, 2024

Euphobia is a Metal Band that incorporates symphonic and synth elements within their music in a unique way.

Contributions:|

I worked with other bandmates to write synth parts and tell a story within the music itself. I provided synth and symphonic elements to the other bandmate's metal instrumentation.

Motivation:|

We all have made music within seperate projects for a while and decided to give it a shot together. We wanted to make metal music but add a fresh and unique twist to what has been done by so many before and around us.

Development:|

We spent a lot of late nights working together to piece together parts together to tell the story we had planned. We also took time to work on the parts on our own time which allowed for creative freedoms to be taken and brought up to the rest of the band later.
