Cannabis Ads|

Overview|

Date: August 9, 2024

Recreational Cannabis Advertisements focusing on Medical Marijuana Brands. I wanted to create something that looked visually appealing and more focused on the product itself.

Contributions:|

I 3D modeledproducts from Real life references, Crafted labels based of existing materials, and created my own spin on advertisments for companies.

Motivation:|

I feel like many brands lacked the visual flair and appealing artwork for their quality products. Many companies neglect or are not allowed this kind of freedoms yet so I wanted to show what I could do.

Development:|

I looked at many existing advertisements for farming and marijuana industries to see what the norms were and what people have already done and also looked at what companies are currently doing for their social presence.

I then went into it, keeping in mind eveything I had learned while also putting my own spin on it and making it into something I would like to see in the world.
